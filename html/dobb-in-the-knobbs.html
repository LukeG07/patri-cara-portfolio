#DobbInTheKnobbs

Our neighbours are breaking lockdown rules. Yet, many remain silent, ignoring the situation and hoping it goes away. However, many people don’t actually like their neighbours with one in three having some sort of dispute with their neighbours. We’re going to show that COVID breaches are actually worse than the other types of bad behaviour we normally put up with and that they should be reported.
