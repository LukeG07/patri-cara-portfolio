#PeriodPoverty

In NZ free sanitary products are only available for students at schools and universities. Yet, those who have finished education still need help. We don’t relate period poverty to adult women who finished school and now have jobs, or are even parents. We’re going to speak to those women directly in our campaign by using thoughts they themselves might have had. We’ll show them that help is there and where they can find it.
